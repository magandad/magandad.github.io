Ordinal Numbers

      Ordinal numbers are expressed with the a- of association.

                  mtoto wa kwanza      ‘first child’

                  mtoto wa pili ‘second child’

                  mtoto wa tatu      ‘third child’:

                  dada wa nne ‘fourth sister’

                  kaka wa tano      ‘fifth brother’

How In the text we have the noun mwezi ‘month’This noun belongs to a class know as m-mi to indicate that m- = singular and mi- is plural. It is also referred to as class 3(singular) and 4(plural). The nouns in this group take wa (singular) and ya (plural)

                  mwezi wa kumi     ‘tenth month’

                  miezi ya kwanza ‘second months’
