The me- tense:

Review the me- tense (in Unit 3 lesson 3). Examples from the text:

Ninyi mmefunga shule kwa muda gain?
‘How long are you in recess from school?’

Mitihani ilikuwa mizuri tu, nimefaulu vizuri.
‘The exams were ok, I (have passed well) did well.’

The question word je:

Je is an interjection type of question word. It can occur alone or suffixed to a verb. For example:

Je, unajifunza Kiswahili? ‘(so) Are you learning Kiswahili?’

Unasemaje ‘boyfriend’ kwa Kiswahili? ‘How do you say ‘boyfriend’ in Kiswahili?’

From the text, we have these examples:

Na wewe je? ‘How about you?’

Mitihani ilikuwaje? ‘How was your exams?’
