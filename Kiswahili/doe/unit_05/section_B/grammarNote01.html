Agreement with numbers and other adjectives:

For numbers, nouns in this group do not take any specific prefix. For example:

radio moja one radio fulana tano five vests
radio mbili two radios fulana tisa nine vests

For adjectives of Bantu origin, an N-prefix is used. For example:

radio nzuri ‘a good radio’ fulana ndogo ‘a small vests’
suruali ndefu ‘long pants’ rangi nyeusi ‘black paint’

They use ya (singular) and za (plural) for prepositions.
For example:

radio ya mwalimu ‘a teache’s radio’ radio za mwalimu ‘teacher’s radios’
suruali ya kazi ‘pants for working’ suruali za kazi ‘pants for working’
