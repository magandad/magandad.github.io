Grammar Note:

About nouns in this unit:
Noun Group:
Review grammar notes in unit 4 and in lesson 1-3 of this unit on nouns in the Ø/N - Ø/N group. Nouns in this unit that fall into that group are:

kalamu ‘pen’ penseli ‘pencil’
rula ‘ruler’ pesa ‘money’

Also nouns in Ø-Ma
maslahi ‘benefits’.

This noun is considered uncountable and always understood in the plural.

The Verb to be in location:
These are -po location near/ familiar to the speaker
-ko location away/unfamiliar from the speaker
-mo inside location

In the reading text, we have the following text:

Duka lake lipo katika mji wa Moshi ‘His shop is in the city of Moshi.’

One would assume that the speaker is either in the city of Moshi or is familiar or has definite information about the shop. Otherwise, one would expect the speaker to use liko instead.
