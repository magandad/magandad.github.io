B.  More Possessive Pronouns

A.  Agreement with numbers and other adjectives:

For numbers, nouns in this unit take the N prefix or 0 prefix. For example:

0 prefix

radio moja one radio                                       fulana tano five vests
radio  mbili two radios                                     fulana tisa nine vests 

N prefix

radio nzuri ‘a good radio’                                           fulana ndogo ‘a small vests’
suruali ndefu  ‘long pants’                         rangi nyeusi ‘black paint’   

 They use ya (singular) and za (plural) for prepositions. For example:

radio ya mwalimu  ‘a teacher’s radio’      radio za mwalimu ‘teacher’s radios’
suruali ya kazi   ‘pants for working’                 suruali za kazi ‘pants for working’
