B.  Tenses (Lessons 1 to 6)

The future tense and future time expressions

The future tense is expressed by the use of the tense marker –ta-:


Utafanya nini kesho? ‘What will you do tomorrow?’
   
Nitakwenda madukani. ‘I will go to the stores.’
   
Kesho ‘tomorrow’
   
Keshokutwa ‘day after tomorrow’
   
Wiki ijayo ‘next week’
   
Baadaye ‘later’

The present tense and present time expressions

The present tense is expressed by the use of the tense marker –na-:

Wiki hii unaingia kazini saa ngapi? 
‘This week, what time you will start work?’


Wiki hii ninaingia kazini asubuhi. 
‘This week I start work in the morning.’

Wiki hii ‘this week’

Sasa ‘now’

The past tense and past time expressions

The past tense is expressed by the use of the tense marker –li-:


Jana ulikwenda wapi? 
‘Where did you go yestewrday?’

 

Jana nilikwenda kazini. 
‘Yesterday, I went to work.’



Jana ‘yesterday’

Wiki iliyopita ‘last week’
